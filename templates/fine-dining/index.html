Chef Laurent Dubois

Executive Chef & Culinary Director

With over 25 years of culinary excellence, Chef Laurent Dubois brings his passion for perfection to every dish. Trained under legendary chefs in Lyon and Paris, he has earned three Michelin stars for his innovative approach to classic French cuisine.

His philosophy is simple: respect the ingredient, honor the tradition, and never stop innovating. Each plate that leaves his kitchen is a testament to his dedication to the culinary arts.
