Our Heritage

Over a century of horological excellence

Swiss Craftsmanship

Every CHRONOS timepiece is a testament to Swiss watchmaking tradition. Our master craftsmen dedicate hundreds of hours to perfecting each component, ensuring that every watch meets our exacting standards.

From the intricate movements to the finest finishing touches, we combine traditional techniques with modern innovation to create timepieces that will last for generations.

  • Hand-assembled by master watchmakers
  • Over 300 individual components per movement
  • Rigorous 28-day testing protocol
  • Lifetime warranty and service support

Timeless Innovation

Since 1892, CHRONOS has been at the forefront of horological innovation. We pioneered several groundbreaking complications and continue to push the boundaries of what's possible in watchmaking.

Our in-house research and development team works tirelessly to develop new materials, movements, and complications that set new standards for the industry.

  • Proprietary automatic movement calibers
  • Advanced anti-magnetic technology
  • Patented perpetual calendar mechanism
  • Innovative shock protection system
